Overview

Snipe-IT was implemented to centralize hardware and software asset tracking, reduce loss, and give IT staff real-time visibility into inventory status.

  • Tracked laptops, desktops, peripherals, and licenses
  • Standardized check-in / check-out procedures
  • Improved accountability for assigned assets
  • Reduced manual tracking and spreadsheet usage

Key Contributions

  • Deployed and configured Snipe-IT for production use
  • Defined asset categories, models, and status labels
  • Implemented user assignment and audit workflows
  • Improved documentation and IT onboarding processes

Impact

  • Improved asset visibility and lifecycle tracking
  • Faster issue resolution and device reassignment
  • More accurate inventory reporting for planning and audits
  • Cleaner, more professional IT operations workflow
